Diamond Dash

a 24-hour minecraft challenge

gameplay

Diamond Dash is a 24-hour Minecraft event where players race to collect as many diamonds as possible before the server closes. Kill your competitors along the way to win bonus rewards like DoorDash gift cards. At the end of the event, the top three players will earn $3 for every diamond in their inventory, so every block counts!

A mining marathon

GameRules

  • 1: once you die - you don't respawn
  • 2: each player is equipped with a built in compass directing them to the nearest player
  • 3: difficulty settings on hard - watch out for those cave mobs!
  • 4: players using hacks like X-ray cheats will be automatically banned upon joining the server
